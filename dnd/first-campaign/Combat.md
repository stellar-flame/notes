# Who goes first:

Each creature rolls:
1d20
plus their Dexterity modifier
Then you put everyone in order from highest to lowest.

## Combat
* the attack bonus to hit
* the damage dice and modifier on a hit
  
# To hit:
* d20 + attack bonus = attack total

attack bonus = ability modifier + proficiency bonus. (usually given with weapon stat for character on sheet)

Then compare to Armor Class

* if equal or higher: hit
* if lower: miss

# Damage:
Damage based on weapon used

* damage die + modifier = damage total

Then subtract from Hit Points.
