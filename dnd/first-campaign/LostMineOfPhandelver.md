## 1. Opening script

See Book Lost Mine Of phandelver Pg 6

## 2. What is actually going on

Keep this in your head:

Gundren and Sildar were ambushed

* goblins captured both of them
* Sildar is at Cragmaw Hideout
* Gundren has already been taken elsewhere
* goblins want to rob, ambush, and protect their hideout

## 3. Important non-player characters

Gundren Rockseeker

* dwarf
* hired the group
* missing
* alive, but taken away

Sildar Hallwinter

* human warrior
* captured in the hideout
* can give useful story direction afterward

Klarg

* bugbear boss
* loud, proud, overconfident
* final threat in the hideout

Yeemik

* goblin in the cave
* selfish and opportunistic
* may bargain with the party

## START

### Scene 1: Road travel

Let players briefly introduce characters.

On the road:

* quiet woods
* tense feeling
* something wrong ahead

### Scene 2: Dead horses

Refer to Pag 6 for description

“Do you want to inspect the horses or look around first?”

* black arrows
* recognizable horses
* signs of struggle
* goblin tracks if investigated

Players may:

* inspect horses
* look for tracks
* search for danger
* move the horses
* ignore them

### Scene 3: Goblin ambush

Hidden goblins attack from the sides.

### Scene 4: Aftermath

Players may:

* search bodies
* follow trail
* capture a goblin
* continue to Phandalin

### Scene 5: Goblin Trail

* Establish marching order

Snare:

* Detect: DC 12 Wisdom Check if Wisodom < 12
* Damage:
  * DC 10 Dexterity OR
  * 1d6 bludgeoning

Pit:

* Passive Wisdom >= 15
* Damage:  
  * DC 10 Dexterity OR
  * 1d6 bludgeoning
