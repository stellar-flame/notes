
# 🇮🇳 Indian-Style Masala Lamb Loin Chops (Baked)

## 🥩 Ingredients

### Marinade
- ½ cup plain yogurt  
- 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste (or fresh grated ginger + garlic)  
- 1 tbsp lemon juice  
- 1 tbsp oil  
- 1 tsp turmeric  
- 1½ tsp garam masala  
- 1 tsp cumin powder  
- 1 tsp coriander powder  
- 1 tsp paprika or Kashmiri chili powder  
- ½–1 tsp chili powder (adjust to taste)  
- Salt (to taste)  

### Optional (recommended)
- 1 tsp kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves, crushed)  
- ½ tsp black pepper  

---

## 🥣 Marination

1. Mix all marinade ingredients in a bowl.
2. Coat lamb loin chops thoroughly.
3. Cover and refrigerate:
   - Minimum: **4 hours**
   - Best: **Overnight**

---

## 🔥 Baking Instructions

1. Preheat oven to **425°F (220°C)**  
2. Place chops on a lined baking tray or rack  
3. Bake for **15–18 minutes**  

### Optional Finish
- Broil for **3–5 minutes** to get a charred, tandoori-style crust  

---

## 🌿 Finishing Touch

- Squeeze fresh lemon juice over chops  
- Garnish with chopped cilantro  

---

## 🌡️ Doneness Guide

- Medium-rare: 130–135°F  
- Medium: 140–145°F  

Rest for **5 minutes** before serving.

---

## 🍽️ Serving Suggestions

- Mint chutney (mint + yogurt + lemon + salt)  
- Naan or roti  
- Onion salad (sliced onions + lemon + salt)  

---

## 🔥 Tips

- Yogurt tenderizes and adds authentic flavor  
- High heat + broil mimics a tandoor oven  
- Avoid overcooking — lamb can become tough quickly  
- For extra smokiness: briefly sear or char after baking
