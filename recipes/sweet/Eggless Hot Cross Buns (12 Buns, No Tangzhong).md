Soft, fluffy hot cross buns without tangzhong.
## Ingredients

### Dough
- 500 g bread flour
- 75 g sugar
- 7 g salt
- 10 g instant yeast
- 2 tsp cinnamon
- 1/4 tsp nutmeg
- 1/4 tsp allspice (optional)
- Zest of 1 orange
- 260 g warm milk
- 80 g plain yogurt
- 1 1/2 tsp vanilla
- 75 g soft butter
- 120 g raisins or currants

### Cross paste
- 60 g flour
- 50–65 g water

### Glaze
- 3 tbsp apricot jam or orange marmalade
- 1 tbsp hot water

## Method
1. Soak fruit in warm water for 10 min. Drain and pat dry.
2. Mix flour, sugar, salt, yeast, spices, and zest. Add milk, yogurt, and vanilla. Mix into a rough dough; rest 10 min.
3. Add butter gradually and knead until smooth and elastic, about 10–12 min. Knead in fruit.
4. Cover and rise in a greased bowl until doubled, 1–1 1/2 hours.
5. Divide into 12, shape into balls, and place in a greased or lined 9 x 13 inch pan.
6. Cover and rise until puffy and touching, 45–75 min.
7. Mix cross paste to a thick pipeable consistency and pipe crosses.
8. Bake at 350°F / 175°C for 22–28 min until golden.
9. Mix glaze and brush over warm buns.

## Tips
- Use bread flour.
- Keep the dough soft and slightly tacky.
- Add butter after the dough comes together.
- Knead well.
- Do not overbake.
- Cover loosely after baking for a softer crust.

## Optional
- Add 15–20 g milk powder for softer buns.
- For active dry yeast, dissolve 10 g in warm milk with a little sugar for 5–10 min first.
