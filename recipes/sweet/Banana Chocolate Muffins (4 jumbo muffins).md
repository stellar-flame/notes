
## Ingredients

- 1 mashed banana (110–130 g)
- 60 g sourdough starter
- 80 g sugar
- 35 g oil
- 1 tsp vanilla
- 60 g flour
- 15 g cocoa *(Dutch Ghirardelli)*
- 1/4 tsp salt

## Rest

- Cover and refrigerate for 8–12 hours.

## Preheat

- Heat oven to **375°F (190°C)**.

## Add after resting

- 1/2 tsp baking soda
- 1/4 tsp baking powder
- Chocolate chips, walnuts, or pecans

## Bake

- Bake for **5 minutes at 375°F**.
- Then bake for **15 minutes at 350°F**.
