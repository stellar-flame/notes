# Pancakes

## Eggless Pancakes (fried in ghee)
*Makes 5–6*

### Ingredients
- 130 g flour
- 15 g sugar
- 2 tsp baking powder
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 180 g milk, room temperature
- 15 g unsalted butter, melted
- 1/2 tsp apple cider vinegar

### Notes
- Fry in ghee.

---

## Pancakes with Egg and Milk Powder (fried in ghee)

### Ingredients
- 120 g flour
- 15 g sugar
- 2 1/3 tsp baking powder
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 1/2 cup milk powder
- 1 cup warm water
- 1 egg
- 15 g butter, melted

### Notes
- Fry in ghee.
